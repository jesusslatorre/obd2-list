What is OBD2?

The so-called OBD (On Board Diagnostics) is a vehicle diagnostic system, such as its acronyms in English indicate it, incorporated into the vehicle and which has the function of controlling and monitoring both to the engine and some other devices; Within these, you can control the level of emissions generated by the unit and determine whether to pollute.

The standard OBD-II interface is not only used by the manufacturer for its functions advanced diagnostics, but also by those that go beyond what the law requires.
